Genius

5 Ways to Train Yourself to Be a Genius (3 Short-term, 2 Long-term)

Genius is a combination of good memory and quality understanding.

  • Becoming a genius is when information has more places to belong, with more relationships, you retain it.
    • Any isolated information isn’t learned efficiently.
    • When your brain sees that it is related to other information, it deems it more relevant and stops trying to forget it as much

    =========================================================

    1. Increase Encoding (memory)
    1. Increase Deep Processing (understanding)
    • Higher Order Learning is the type of thinking that creates relationships, meaning, structure and depth to understanding information.
      • How does this information relate to other information? How is is similar or different to what I’m learning or what I already know?
    • Lower Order Leaning is the lower level learning that seems easier on the short term
  • related → grouping → chunking → comparing

===========================================================
