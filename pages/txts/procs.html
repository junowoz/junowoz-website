Procastination

Psychology shows procrastination is caused by a number of reasons.

Here is a few that you may be suffering from:

  • Anxiety • Fear of failure • Lack of structure • Negative thoughts • Lack of self-confidence

Here are 8 tricks to tackle them and defeat procrastination:

  1. Prioritize

Make sure you address the most critical or time-sensitive assignments first.

Get the hard stuff out of the way first, so that everything that comes after it will seem more manageable.

  1. Meditate

Meditation is known to get rid of;

  • Stress • Anxiety • Fear of failure • Negative thoughts

This will allow you to have self-belief and you will be able to take action without fear.

  1. Set Goals

It's a lot easier to;

  • Take action • Have motivation • Believe in yourself

When you have goals to work towards. Establish simple, reachable goals rather than face a big, vague plan.

  1. Journal

Journaling can allow you to;

  • Let go of negative feelings • Express your emotions out • Track your daily/weekly progress

This way you can look back and realise all the progress you've been making. You'll have something to be proud of.

  1. Take breaks

Taking breaks to mentally + physically recharge will allow you to;

  • Avoid burn out • Stay motivated • Stay disciplined • Improve productivity

Taking breaks relieve stress, meaning and have a better mental health and well-being.

  • Plan your day

Planning your day will give you a structure to follow.

This will help;

  • Relieve stress • Improve motivation • Increase productivity

This means you aren't stressing, worring and overthinking about what to do, and what not to do.

  • Hold yourself accountable

Holding yourself accountable allows you to prioritize your goals and better understand the reasons you have them.

You can use accountability strategies to motivate yourself and learn more about your work process.
